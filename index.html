How to do the Infinity Pen Trick

Be bored in class but with style. You will get all the friends in class.

Step 1: Holding the Pen/Pencil

Hold the pen/pencil like usual but go towards the edge of the pencil but not the tip.

Step 2: Move the Pen/Pencil

Flick the pen/pencil downwards and catch it with your middle finger.

Step 3: Finish it and Repeat

Then the tip of the pen/pencil will go in between your middle and index finger. Then you would catch it with your thumb and bring it back to your starting point. Then Repeat it to do the Infinity Pen Trick.
